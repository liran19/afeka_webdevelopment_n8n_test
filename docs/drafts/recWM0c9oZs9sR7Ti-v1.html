Japan Travel Guide

Discover the Land of the Rising Sun

Tokyo Skytree

Tokyo Skytree is a broadcasting tower that offers breathtaking views of the city. At 634 meters tall, it's the tallest tower in the world.

Fushimi Inari Shrine

Fushimi Inari Shrine is famous for its thousands of vermillion torii gates that form a tunnel up the mountain. It's a unique and breathtaking sight.

Osaka Castle

Osaka Castle is a majestic castle that played a crucial role in the unification of Japan. It's now a museum and offers great views of the city.
