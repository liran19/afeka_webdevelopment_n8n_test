Explore Paris

Attraction 1: Eiffel Tower

The Eiffel Tower is a must-visit attraction in Paris, offering stunning views of the city. It's located at 48.8583, 2.2945.

Attraction 2: Louvre Museum

The Louvre Museum is one of the world's largest and most famous museums, housing an impressive collection of art and artifacts. It's located at 48.8607, 2.2943.

Attraction 3: Seine River Cruise

A Seine River cruise is a great way to see the city's landmarks while learning about its history and culture. It's located at 48.8567, 2.3508.

Attraction 4: Arc de Triomphe

The Arc de Triomphe is a iconic monument that honors the soldiers who fought and died for France. It's located at 48.8739, 2.2953.

Attraction 5: Luxembourg Gardens

The Luxembourg Gardens are a beautiful and peaceful oasis in the heart of the city, perfect for a stroll or a picnic. It's located at 48.8467, 2.3372.
