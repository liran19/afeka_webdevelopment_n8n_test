Paris Attractions

Eiffel Tower

The Eiffel Tower is a must-visit attraction in Paris, offering stunning views of the city. It's a great spot for a romantic dinner or a family outing.

Coordinates: 48.8583, 2.2945

Champs-Élysées

The Champs-Élysées is one of the most famous streets in the world, lined with cafes, shops, and theaters. It's a great place to people-watch and enjoy the city's energy.

Coordinates: 48.8697, 2.3078

Le Comptoir du Relais

Le Comptoir du Relais is a classic Parisian bistro, serving up delicious French cuisine in a cozy atmosphere. It's a great spot for a romantic dinner or a night out with friends.

Coordinates: 48.8533, 2.2967

The Louvre

The Louvre is one of the world's largest and most famous museums, housing an impressive collection of art and artifacts from around the world. It's a must-visit attraction for art lovers and history buffs.

Coordinates: 48.8607, 2.2945

Luxembourg Gardens

Luxembourg Gardens is a beautiful and tranquil oasis in the heart of the city, offering stunning views, beautiful gardens, and plenty of walking trails.

Coordinates: 48.8467, 2.3378
