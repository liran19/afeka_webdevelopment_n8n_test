Israel Attractions

Discover the best places to visit in Israel

Tel Aviv Beach

Enjoy the sun, sand, and sea at Tel Aviv's beautiful beach. Take a stroll along the promenade and enjoy the city's vibrant atmosphere.

Coordinates: 32.0711, 34.7683

Jerusalem Old City

Explore the historic Old City of Jerusalem, with its narrow streets, ancient walls, and sacred sites. Visit the Western Wall, the Church of the Holy Sepulchre, and the Dome of the Rock.

Coordinates: 31.7833, 35.2167

Haifa Bahai Gardens

Visit the stunning Bahai Gardens in Haifa, a UNESCO World Heritage Site. The gardens are a beautiful example of landscaping and architecture, with stunning views of the city.

Coordinates: 32.8167, 34.9833

Dead Sea

Float in the Dead Sea, the lowest point on Earth, and enjoy the unique experience of weightlessness. Relax on the beach and take in the stunning scenery.

Coordinates: 31.5333, 35.6167

Masada

Visit the ancient fortress of Masada, a UNESCO World Heritage Site. Learn about the history of the site and take in the breathtaking views of the Dead Sea.

Coordinates: 31.3167, 35.35
