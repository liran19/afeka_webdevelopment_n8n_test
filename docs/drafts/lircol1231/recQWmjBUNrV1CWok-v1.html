Discover Japan

Tokyo Skytree

Tokyo Skytree is a broadcasting tower that offers breathtaking views of the city. At 634 meters tall, it's the tallest tower in the world, and its observation decks are a must-visit for any traveler.

Lat: 35.7101, Lng: 139.8089

Fushimi Inari Shrine

Fushimi Inari Shrine is famous for its thousands of vermilion torii gates that form a tunnel up the mountain. It's a unique and breathtaking sight that's steeped in history and culture.

Lat: 34.9673, Lng: 135.7724

Universal Studios Japan

Universal Studios Japan is a theme park that's perfect for families and fans of movies and TV shows. With its exciting rides and attractions, it's a great place to spend the day.

Lat: 34.6653, Lng: 135.4331
