Discover Paris

Eiffel Tower

Visit the iconic Eiffel Tower for breathtaking views of the city. It's a must-visit attraction in Paris.

Champs-Élysées

Stroll along the famous Champs-Élysées and enjoy the street performers, cafes, and shopping. It's a great place to people-watch and soak up the Parisian atmosphere.

Coordinates: 48.8697, 2.3078

Le Comptoir du Relais

Indulge in delicious French cuisine at Le Comptoir du Relais, a cozy bistro with a warm atmosphere. Try their famous escargots and steak tartare.

Coordinates: 48.8534, 2.2955

Louvre Museum

Visit the world-famous Louvre Museum and see the Mona Lisa, Venus de Milo, and other famous artworks. The museum's stunning architecture and beautiful gardens are also worth exploring.

Coordinates: 48.8607, 2.2945

Luxembourg Gardens

Escape the hustle and bustle of the city and relax in the beautiful Luxembourg Gardens. The gardens feature stunning fountains, statues, and walking paths.

Coordinates: 48.8467, 2.3378
