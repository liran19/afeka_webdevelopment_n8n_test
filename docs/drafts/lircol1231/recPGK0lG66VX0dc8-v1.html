Israel Attractions

Explore the best places in Israel

Tel Aviv Beach

Relax on the beautiful beaches of Tel Aviv, enjoy the sun and the sea. Tel Aviv is known for its vibrant atmosphere and stunning coastline.

Coordinates: 32.0711, 34.7633

Jerusalem Old City

Explore the historic Old City of Jerusalem, with its rich history and cultural significance. Visit the Western Wall, the Church of the Holy Sepulchre, and the Dome of the Rock.

Coordinates: 31.7833, 35.2167

Haifa Bahai Gardens

Visit the stunning Bahai Gardens in Haifa, a UNESCO World Heritage Site. The gardens are a beautiful example of landscape design and offer breathtaking views of the city.

Coordinates: 32.8167, 34.9833

Dead Sea Landscape

Explore the unique landscape of the Dead Sea, with its salt formations and stunning views. Float in the salty waters and enjoy the therapeutic benefits of the Dead Sea.

Coordinates: 31.5333, 35.6167

Masada Fortress

Visit the ancient fortress of Masada, a UNESCO World Heritage Site. The fortress is situated on a rocky plateau and offers breathtaking views of the surrounding landscape.

Coordinates: 31.3167, 35.35
