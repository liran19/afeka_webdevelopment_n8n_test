Louvre Museum

The Louvre Museum is one of the world's largest and most famous museums, housing a vast collection of art and artifacts from around the world. Located in the heart of Paris, the museum is a must-visit destination for anyone interested in art, history, and culture.

Eiffel Tower

The Eiffel Tower is an iconic symbol of Paris and one of the most recognizable landmarks in the world. Built for the 1889 World's Fair, the tower stands at an impressive 324 meters tall and offers breathtaking views of the city.

Lat: 48.8583, Lng: 2.2945

Notre-Dame Cathedral

Notre-Dame Cathedral is a beautiful and historic church located on the Île de la Cité in Paris. Built in the 12th century, the cathedral is known for its stunning architecture, stunning stained glass windows, and rich history.
