Japan Travel Guide

Discover the Land of the Rising Sun

Tokyo Skytree

Tokyo Skytree is a broadcasting tower that offers panoramic views of the city. At 634 meters tall, it is the tallest tower in the world.

Fushimi Inari Shrine

Fushimi Inari Shrine is famous for its thousands of vermilion torii gates that form a tunnel up the mountain. It is a beautiful and iconic spot for hiking and photography.

Osaka Castle

Osaka Castle is a majestic castle that played a crucial role in the unification of Japan. It now serves as a museum and offers great views of the city.
