Paris Attractions

Eiffel Tower

The Eiffel Tower is a must-visit attraction in Paris. It offers stunning views of the city from its top.

Coordinates: 48.8583, 2.2945

Louvre Museum

The Louvre Museum is one of the world's largest and most famous museums. It houses an impressive collection of art and artifacts.

Coordinates: 48.8607, 2.2945

Notre-Dame Cathedral

Notre-Dame Cathedral is a beautiful and historic church that offers stunning views of the city from its towers.

Coordinates: 48.8567, 2.3003
