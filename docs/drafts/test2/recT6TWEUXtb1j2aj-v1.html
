Paris Attractions

Eiffel Tower

The Eiffel Tower is a must-visit attraction in Paris, offering stunning views of the city. It's a great spot to learn about the history and engineering of the tower.

Coordinates: 48.8583, 2.2945

Louvre Museum

The Louvre Museum is one of the world's largest and most famous museums, housing an impressive collection of art and artifacts. Be sure to check out the Mona Lisa.

Coordinates: 48.8607, 2.3231

Seine River Cruise

A Seine River cruise is a great way to see the sights of Paris while learning about its history and culture. You'll pass by many famous landmarks and bridges.

Coordinates: 48.8567, 2.3508
